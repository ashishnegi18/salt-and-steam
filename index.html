From the notebook

Cooking on a Tuesday, not a Sunday.

Most food writing is built for the weekend — the long simmer, the three-bowl mise en place, the photogenic mess. This notebook is for the other five days: the ones where dinner has to happen between a meeting and a load of laundry.

Every recipe here is tested on a weeknight, in a kitchen with exactly enough counter space and never enough hands. Bold flavor, short ingredient lists, and a pan you only have to wash once.

— written, tested, and occasionally burnt by one very stubborn home cook.

15–40 min, most nights
1 pan, when possible
0 ingredients you can’t pronounce
